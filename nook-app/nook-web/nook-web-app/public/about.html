Nook

Nook is a client-side password and secrets manager for encrypted vaults that stay under your control.

Create a vault in your browser, protect it with this device's keys, and connect optional sync providers such as Google Drive or GitHub to replicate encrypted vault data. Nook does not run a central account service and cannot read your secrets.

Open Nook Read privacy policy

Client-side encryption

Secrets are encrypted in your browser before they are saved or synced.

Your storage

Keep a local vault first, then optionally sync encrypted vault data through providers you choose.

Optional Google Drive sync

When you connect Google Drive, Nook uses app-data storage for its own encrypted vault files only.
